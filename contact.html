Frequently Asked Questions

For Prospective Students

Q: Are you accepting new Ph.D. students?
A: Yes, I typically accept 1-2 new Ph.D. students each year. Please review my research areas and recent publications before reaching out.
Q: What should I include in my inquiry email?
A: Please include your CV, research interests, relevant experience, and specific reasons for wanting to work with me.
Q: Do you offer undergraduate research opportunities?
A: Yes, I work with motivated undergraduates on research projects. Strong programming skills and relevant coursework are preferred.

For Collaborators

Q: What types of collaborations are you interested in?
A: I'm open to academic collaborations, industry partnerships, and interdisciplinary projects related to Combinatorial Optimisation and Operations Research.
Q: Do you consult for industry?
A: I occasionally provide consulting services for projects aligned with my research expertise. Please email for details.
Q: Are you available for speaking engagements?
A: Yes, I speak at conferences, workshops, and industry events. Please provide event details and timeline when inquiring.
